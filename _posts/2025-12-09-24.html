  • Steve Symons

    The Light That Entangles:
    Following on from his previous Entangled Instrument talk Steve will present elements of his latest three person instrument based on audio-encoded light. He will talk through and demonstrate the technology behind the system and explore how his approach fits into a new trend in HCI for digital musical instruments.
